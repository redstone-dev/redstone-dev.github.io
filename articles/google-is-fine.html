Why Google is Actually Not So Bad

Now, that probably sounds like the stuff that comes out of a bull's rear end. But it's true.

Google is not as bad as you might think.

There are a lot of reasons for this, but I'll give you the big one.

They only collect data on you from their proprietary services. So they track you when you use Google Chrome or Google Search, but not Chromium, because Chromium is not proprietary, while Chrome is.

Also, while they do have a large reach across the internet, if you use stuff like uBO, Decentraleyes or DDG Privacy Essentials, I'd say 99-100% of their tracking power is eliminated.

Finally, just be sensible with what you share online. It's a good rule of thumb anyways.
